Fully configurable bill presentment service
BillVia offers complete line of bill presentment services conforming with Interactive Financial Exchange (IFX) standards. BillVia can be configured to
 -receive and process electronic and paper notifications of bill-able services
 -provide specialized pricing functions such as freight rates and customer contracts
 -provide specialized promotion functions such as clubs, co-ops, enrollments, withdrawals
 -provide complete interface (billing and payment) with payment service providers via on-line requests, emails, EDI, ftp and so on


Different types of Billto, Electronic Bills or Paper Bills
BillVia can handle different types of customers whether they are standard customer or one time customers. The bills produced may be electronic - EDI, IFX/OFX Invoices, XML transfers, Email attachments or plain paper or fax invoices.

Source Documents - Electronic or Paper
Timesheets, Shipping documents, Bill of Ladings, Packing Lists or any such document can become the billing document - and BillVia can work handle it. You may email,fax, ftp, http,EDI or use plain mail.


Specialized Rating and Verification  Facility
Features such as price lookup, promotions and contract verifications can be configured.
Freight Rating Specialty utilizes advanced rating scheme of ShipVia to verify each and every shipment for customer contracts and/or published tariffs.  BOL and/or shipment data can be configured to be extracted from another system.
Promotions Specialty utilizes PriceVia's Rewards scheme to handle specialized accounting of Co-ops, banks, Spifs, club enrollments as so on.


Billing Authorizations, Cycle billing, Anniversary Billing Options
Regular, Anniversary, Cycle or Recurring billing can be setup to provide full flexibility in timing, content and delivery. Generated bills can be sent to you for authorization before they  can be released.


Analysis
Do cash flow analysis, what if analysis, generate cost codes, book accruals  - it is all easy.


Advanced  Features, New Technology
Advanced features incorporating three tier architecture and XML interfaces allow  true scaleability. Design features IFX compliant architecture allowing collaborative processing with various service providers and B2B exchanges. A host of schemes to interact with your systems employing HTTP, SMTP(email), FTP,EDI or XML protocols.  Easy integration with legacy systems.
