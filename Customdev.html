Custom Development

 

Custom Development contracts ,when managed under strict controls, can save the organization time and money. 

Benefits

Typically, custom development done offshore, can have cost savings amounting to 50% due to the cost differential in wage structures and govermental incentives.

Availability of skilled manpower to staff up to suit the project's requirements, gives the Project Manager more flexibility.

The twelve hour time differential between US and Asia enables   round the clock attention to the project. 

Risk Factors

Security and Confidentiality  may be compromised by unscrupulous vendors.

Changes to specifications are more difficult to incorporate

The delivered product differs in quality.

Guarantees and other clauses are difficult to enforce

Synergy Software Methodology

Synergy Software provides a reliable and foolproof method to eliminate the risks in Offshore development while guaranteeing the benefits by

-Controlling the projects and supplementing the development effort from the US.

-Using vendors who are affiliated to Synergy. These vendors are contractually obligated to honor Synergy's confidentiality agreements.

Successful Offshore projects

A wide spectrum of projects - Point Of Sale Systems, Communication Systems, E-Commerce Systems, Web Site Development, Y2K remedial, Credit Card Processing System, File Mapping Utilities, Remote Customer Assistance Modules and so on- have been developed offshore for Synergy's Clients.
