Regie Paul to join Synergy Software

New Jersey, September 20, 2000.   Synergy Software and its affiliates will have the services of Mr Regie Paul from October 1, 2000. Mr Paul is leaving his current assignment as the Chief Executive in the Alcan Aluminum Company's Indian operation, Indian Aluminium Company popularly known as INDAL.

Mr Paul will bring with him his varied experience in organization building. As a Chief Executive in INDAL, he had major share in turning the businesses around, which were beset with labor problems and increased competition. He is credited with having been able negotiate the first ever productivity based wage system in a public limited company in the State of Kerala, and directing the unit to profitability.

At Synergy, the job is cut out for him, his primary goal is to build the organization. Mr Paul has the right background to do just that. After a degree in Mechanical Engineering from the prestigious IIT Madras, Regie started his career with the largest truck manufacturing facility - Tata Engineering. After a four year stint at TELCO, Poona, Regie joined INDAL where he stayed on for 18 years to become a Chief Executive.

Regie continued to perfect his management style combined with acquisition of knowledge. He has been awarded several degrees and diplomas in management including MBA from Cochin University, Master of Management degree from McGill University in Canada, Diplomas from Indian Institute of Management Bangalore, Lancaster University, UK and universities in Japan and France.

Initially, Regie will take responsibility for Clarion Systems Pvt Lld. as Managing Director. He will also direct Synergy Software Corporation's plans as its Chief Operating Officer(Acting). He will be directly responsible for launching the ASP offerings from Synergy - BillVia,PayVia and ShipVia.
