Fully Configurable Payment Service
PayVia offers complete line of payment services conforming to Interactive Financial Exchange [IFX] standards. PayVia may be configured to function as:
  .  Payment Servicer to handle all electronic and paper bills
  .  Biller Payment Service Provider to receive payment
  .  Specialized Bill Payment Service Provider with auditing services such as freight audit and three way matches
  .  Interface with Banking Service Provider
  .  Broker to provide authorization and draft capture with credit card companies.

All Payees, Providers and Billers - Fully Customizable Options
PayVia can handle different types of payees whether they are standard billing service providers or one time payees. The bills may be electronic - EDI, IFX/OFX Invoices, XML transfers, e-mail attachments or plain paper, or fax invoices. PayVia scans paper invoices and an online image is always available for audit.

Audit Facility
PayVia's Audit features are superior to dedicated bill payment solutions. Audit features are fully configurable, and include options such as three-way match, freight audit, price verifications and contract verifications.
Freight Audit Specialty: utilizes advanced freight rating scheme of ShipVia to verify each and every shipment for customer contracts and/or published tariffs.  BOL and/or shipment data is extracted from the customer' s system.

 

Payment Authorizations and Pay-from Options
Authorization schemes are configurable. PayVia offers the convenience of applying standard rules or your personal authorization of each bill. You select the mode of communication - e-mails, spreadsheets or webpages. Pay from different accounts or even using credit cards.


Accounting Analysis
Do cash requirement analysis, what-if analysis, generate cost codes, book accruals.  PayVia makes it easy.


Advanced  Features, New Technology
Advanced features incorporating three tier architecture and XML interfaces allow  true scalability. IFX-compliant design features and architecture allow collaborative processing with various service providers and B2B exchanges. A host of schemes to interact with your systems employing HTTP, SMTP[e-mail], FTP, EDI or XML protocols.  PayVia utilizes advanced technology to put you ahead; even while it offers the convenience of easy integration with legacy systems.
