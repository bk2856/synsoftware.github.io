SHIPVIA is released for beta testing

New York, July 31, 2000.   Synergy Software Corporation announced today that the SHIPVIA product is available for beta testing. The product will be hosted on SHIPVIA.COM portal and will be available to public in the second quarter..

SHIPVIA uses Synergy's time tested rating engine which is available for both AS/400 and Oracle platform. Besides rating SHIPVIA allows the users to perform the complete set of shipping transactions including

Pre-shipment planning, pre-rating, shipping schedule, carrier selection, shipping documents generation, carrier scheduling, tracking, and freight payment and analysis.

The details of SHIPVIA deployment are still worked out.

Synergy Software is a strategic application development company located in Maywood, New Jersey. It specializes in business application development using the new technologies. Synergy has technology partnership agreements with IBM, BEA Systems, Oracle, Progress, Netscape and Microsoft.  Recent clients include 1800Flowers, Lenox China, Reliable Automatic Sprinkler, G-III Apparel, Workbench, and T-FAL.
