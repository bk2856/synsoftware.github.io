Synergy to use SMC's CZARLITE scheme

Maywood, December 20, 2000.   Synergy Software signed an agreement with SMC in Atlanta. Under this agreement Synergy Software will incorporate SMC's CZARLITE rating Scheme into the Synergy Freight Management System.

Synergy plans to use the CZARLITE DLL scheme and interface with its rating scheme which is Java based. The AS400 based rating engine also will be extended to connect to the NT based DLL.

Synergy FMS allows the customers to setup and maintain any tariff and agreement of their liking. The rates for all different modes including TL, LTL, Parcel, Postal, Ocean, Air, Rail, mail and Contract rates are maintained in one single database giving customers one standard interface. However, it is felt that by incorporating the popular CZARLITE program and data modules, the customers' overall maintenance costs will be come down. By tightly integrating the CZARLITE DLL and data into the system, the customer now has a choice of maintaining the tariff data by themselves or subscribing to SMCs data modules.

The CZARLITE integration will be completed by January 2001.
