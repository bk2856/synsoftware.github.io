Synergy becomes Allaire's Technology Partner

New Jersey, October 13, 2000.   Synergy Software has been accepted by Allaire as its technology partner. Under this new partnership, Synergy will be converting some of its products to work with the industry standard servers and tools provided by Allaire.

Allaire Alliance Technology Partners include pre-eminent software and hardware vendors who share Allaire’s vision of bringing business to the Web. By offering commercial applications built on Allaire technology and products complementary to the Allaire Business Platform, these partners extend and enhance the high-quality offerings Allaire customers expect.

Synergy has been utilizing Allaire's products such as Homesite and Cold Fusion for a long period of time. The application server product JRun, which is J2EE compliant and EJB enabled, has been selected as a server platform to be compatable with Synergy's product lines ShipVvia, BillVia, and PayVia. Currently, these products have been tested for several servers including IBM WebSphere, Bea WebLogic, Appache (servlet support required), and Netscape Enterprise Server.

Synergy believes that Allaire's products are packed with functionality, quality and value. Synergy intends to make use all products and tools offered by Allaire.
