Powerful Customized Shipping Solutions
It is no longer necessary to maintain in-house shipping software to derive the benefits of carrier selection, shipment planning, routing or customized document preparation. All the benefits of such expensive software are now  available on the Internet at a fraction of the cost. Plus additional features for your business to compete better in the industry!


All Carriers, Modes and Rates - Fully Customizable Rating
ShipVia will find the right shipping service for your needs. Whether it be common carrier,  dedicated carrier,  your own fleet, rail, mail,  or parcel, ShipVia keeps track of  all the rates and options. ShipVia can handle the FAKs, floors, discounts, minimums, surcharges, accessorials , transit times,  market lanes, and service levels. Your shipping points, zones and bulk breaks - every thing is configurable. You are guaranteed to get the best carrier that matches your criteria - whether it be  the cheapest or the fastest or anything else.  Plus, you can opt to invite bids. The best carrier and the best rate - that is what ShipVia will give.

Shipment Planning, Consolidation, Pickup Appointment 
ShipVia will help you with advance planning of shipments based on configurable policies. With advanced shipment planning, you can save money and enhance efficiency. Also, ShipVia gives you the ability to consolidate shipments to suit your cost structure. You can also setup the pickup appointment and manage pickup schedule on-line.


Customized Shipping Documents Available
With ShipVia, there is no longer a need for you to custom-program every document. All the documents you require are available already. Customizable templates exist to generate a wide variety of printed forms. ShipVia can customize even packing labels with bar codes and tracking numbers and send the printable document to your packer in the warehouse.

Shipment Tracking, Claims, Freight Billing and Payment
Want to track a shipment? You can trace it on-line. ShipVia also allows to manage your freight claims and carrier communications. Want to take advantage of self billing? Or need to generate freight invoices for a customer? ShipVia makes it easy.


Freight Analysis
Do what-if analysis, do transshipment analysis, or just compare carriers. Generate cost codes, or book freight accruals to flexible GL codes. ShipVia gives you all the flexibility. 

Advanced  Features, New Technology
Advanced features incorporating three tier architecture and XML interfaces allow  true scalability.  IFX-compliant architecture allows collaborative processing with various service providers and B2B exchanges.  ShipVia includes a host of schemes to interact with your systems employing HTTP, SMTP[e-mail], FTP,EDI or XML protocols. ShipVia utilizes advanced technology to put you ahead, even while it offers an easy integration with your current systems.
