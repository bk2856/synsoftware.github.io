Synergy Introduces Easy Accounting System for small businesses and families

.   Synergy Software has released a new integrated accounting module for businesses and families. This software differs from typical systems in several ways.

Software allows to set up goals and strategies and compare with actuals

It follows a multidimensional chart of accounts

Its is simple in operation, just one set of books allowing for eight different types of transactions
1.cash in
2.cash out
3.material in
4.material out
5.Invoice
6.Voucher
7.Non cash transaction
8.Adjustments/Deposits

Each of these transaction can be programmed to inteact with asset(inventory/fixed assets), offsets(accounts/ar/ap) or accumulators (such as time, goodwill, cost etc.) Full Featured demo sites are planned now.
