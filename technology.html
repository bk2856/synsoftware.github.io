Advances in Technology and the future

Advances in Technology have been rapid - communication technology and semiconductor technology are radically altering the industrial landscape. The Internet has been a watershed of innovation and productivity. The advent of Global Village and the associated productivity gains demand that older systems and procedures be supplanted with innovative schemes. To keep up with the pace of technology, Synergy spends sizable resources in in-house development and training; and products with higher technology content are subsidized for technology sake.
