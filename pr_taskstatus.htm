Sample uses of Request Management

Service calls, customer complaints, purchasing functions, Program changes, service calls, recruitment tasks, Outside contracts, subject tracking and so on.

Initiated by a user, the request typically gets analyzed. It may get rejected or accepted to be worked on. Estimates and detail documents may be generated to proceed to the next level. The department responsible for resolving the task, normally creates a project to handle it. The resolution of the issue involves assigning the detailed tasks to various resources and monitoring them. Time, manpower as well as other tangible resources are consumed during the course of the resolution process.

Some issues may need a lots of communication back and forth. We can start a note-stream to direct the communications to specific subjects. And it is possible to have any number of such notes.

Multi Platform
