       Synergy Software Corporation provides strategic information technology solutions to medium and large sized corporations. Synergy also develops packaged software solutions to be used in distribution, sales and e-Commerce marketplace.

Synergy Software started operations in 1986 as a consulting outfit. Synergy Software Corporation was incorporated in 1992.
